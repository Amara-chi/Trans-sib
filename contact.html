Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I get a shipping quote?

You can request a quote through our contact form above, call our sales team, or use our online quote calculator on our website. We'll respond within 4 hours with a detailed quote.

What information do I need for shipping?

We need origin and destination details, cargo dimensions and weight, type of goods, preferred service level, and any special handling requirements. Our team will guide you through the process.

Do you provide tracking services?

Yes, we provide real-time tracking for all shipments through our digital platform. You'll receive tracking details and regular updates throughout the shipping process.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including credit accounts for established customers, prepayment options, and various payment methods including wire transfers and credit cards.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive customs brokerage services including documentation preparation, duty calculation, and clearance procedures for import and export shipments.

What is your response time for emergencies?

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 and typically responds to urgent situations within 30 minutes. We have dedicated emergency contact numbers for critical issues.
