Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our company

How long has GlobalLogistics been in business?

GlobalLogistics has been serving clients worldwide since 1999, bringing over 25 years of expertise in international logistics and supply chain management.

What makes GlobalLogistics different from competitors?

Our combination of cutting-edge technology, global network coverage, sustainability focus, and personalized customer service sets us apart. We invest heavily in AI and digital solutions while maintaining a human touch.

How many countries do you operate in?

We have a presence in over 150 countries with 500+ office locations, ensuring local expertise and global reach for all your logistics needs.

What sustainability initiatives does GlobalLogistics have?

We're committed to carbon-neutral shipping by 2030, investing in green technologies, optimizing routes for fuel efficiency, and partnering with eco-friendly carriers.

How can I track my shipments?

Our advanced digital platform provides real-time tracking with detailed status updates, GPS location data, and predictive delivery estimates accessible 24/7.
