Our Sustainability Initiatives

Comprehensive programs driving measurable environmental impact across our global operations

Clean Energy Transition

Transitioning to 100% renewable energy across all facilities through solar, wind, and geothermal solutions. Our 35MW solar array at the Rotterdam terminal is the largest in the logistics industry.

87% Complete 2025 Target

Green Fleet Program

Electrifying 100% of our last-mile delivery vehicles and implementing hydrogen fuel cell technology for long-haul transport. Currently operating 1,200 electric vehicles across 15 countries.

68% Complete 2026 Target

Zero Waste Operations

Achieving 95% waste diversion from landfills through advanced recycling programs and circular economy partnerships. Implemented in 22 facilities with plans for global rollout.

74% Complete 2027 Target

Sustainable Community Partnerships

Investing $25 million annually in local environmental initiatives, urban reforestation, and clean transportation infrastructure in communities where we operate.

92% Complete 2024 Target
